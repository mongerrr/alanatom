Travelling to our Wedding

St Mary Star of the Sea is located in the main town of Gerringong, with Crooked River Winery only a short 4 minute drive away.

There will be plenty of on-site parking at both locations.

For those wishing to stay local, there are plenty of accomodation options in Gerringong, Gerroa, Kiama and Berry, just to name a few locations within 15 minutes of the venue.
